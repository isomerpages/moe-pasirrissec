### Let's do Crafting and Coding!

At Tinkers' Club, students learn about crafting and coding. As we live in an ICT-savvy environment, it is essential for students to learn coding and understand the basic fundamentals of creating programmes using block coding software and microcontrollers. Crafting is another essential skill that we want students to acquire. Students will learn to be creative and to innovate their own prototypes using modelling materials and crafting equipment. These programmes allow students to be future-ready, where innovation and basic ICT knowledge are essential in their daily lives.
