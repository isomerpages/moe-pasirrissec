<h3><strong>Mavis Ho – Staff Achievement</strong></h3>
<h6><a href="http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/teachers-awarded-for-inventive-ways-in-bringing-current-affairs-to-classrooms" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Teachers awarded for inventive ways in bringing current affairs to classrooms</a></h6>
<p>SINGAPORE - Four teachers received the inaugural Singapore Press Holdings'
(SPH) Innovative Educators Award at the SPH Education Symposium 2017 on
Monday (Aug 28). The award recognises teachers who have found inventive
ways in the classroom to hone their students' current affairs knowledge
and life skills using the news.</p>
