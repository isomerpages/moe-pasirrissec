<p><strong>Step 4:</strong> Select Subject Combinations which include the
relevant elective subjects</p>
<p>At upper secondary level, your child/ward will have to offer a set of
compulsory subjects. In addition to these, your child/ward may choose between
1-3 elective subjects. Based on the courses and prerequisite subjects shortlisted
in Step 3, identify the relevant Subject Combinations. You can click the
links below to view the Subject Combinations available and find out more
about the subjects.
