“Time to Dream” is a vibrant showcase that highlights the talents and dedication of our student performers. This year’s production weaves together a shared narrative of a boy’s journey towards discovering his dreams and purpose, reflecting the challenges and aspirations many young people face. Join us for an unforgettable evening as all five PRSS Performing Arts CCAs unite on stage in a powerful celebration of talent, passion, and creativity.
