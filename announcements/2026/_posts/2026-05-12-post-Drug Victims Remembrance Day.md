<p>From 12 to 14 May 2026, the school commemorated Drug Victims Remembrance
Day to create awareness of how family and society are also victims of drug
abuse, other than the abusers, and encourage community support for the
anti-drug cause. The commemoration began with a sharing on the impact of
drug abuse on family and society during assembly to sensitize students
to how drug abuse affects people other than the abusers and that it is
not victimless.&nbsp; This was followed by classroom discussions and activities
on the impact of drug abuse on others and how to support loved ones who
may be abusing substances. We hope these activities reinforce the importance
adopting healthy coping strategies and to stay away from abusing substances
like drugs. These are photos of students and staff engaged in the Drug
Victims Remembrance Day activities.</p>
