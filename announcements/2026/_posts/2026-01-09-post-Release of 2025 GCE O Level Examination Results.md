Dear Students and Parents/Guardians of the 2025 Graduating Cohort,

The 2025 GCE O-Level Examination Results will be released on **14 January 2026 (Wednesday) at 2.00 pm**.

All 4Exp and 5NA students are encouraged to return to school to collect their results. A briefing on post-secondary application options will be conducted after the release of results.

Parents/Guardians are welcome to accompany their child/ward.

##### **Reporting Details**

•	**Reporting Time:** 1.30 pm<br>
•	**Reporting Venue**: School Hall<br>
•	**Attire &amp; Grooming:** <br>Students are required to be in full or half school uniform and adhere to school grooming rules. Students who are inappropriately attired will be denied entry into the school.

##### **Important Reminders (prior to collection of results)**

All students are required to:<br>
•	Settle any outstanding school and/or examination fees at the General Office<br>
•	Return all library books to the General Office

##### **Collection of Results by Proxy**

Students who are unable to return to school on 14 January 2026 to collect their results must appoint a proxy to do so on their behalf. Students are required to inform their Home Teachers of the details of the proxy’s (name, relationship, and contact number) by **13 January 2026 (Tuesday), 12.00 pm.**

We wish all our students every success in their future endeavors.
